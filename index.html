About Us

At Hastigo, we are committed to providing safe, comfortable, and affordable vehicle pooling services across cities. Our mission is to reduce road congestion and promote sustainable travel through efficient carpooling solutions.


We offer a variety of services, including bike taxis and car rides across cities, with options ranging from hatchbacks, sedans, and SUVs to premium cars for those looking for added comfort. Our goal is to ensure that you have the flexibility to choose the vehicle that suits your needs, whether you're traveling for business, leisure, or personal errands.

In addition, we provide airport pick-up and drop services throughout the city, ensuring that you arrive at your destination on time and with ease.


Hastigo Share helps connect users and vehicle owners for ride-pooling on outstation journeys, allowing travelers to share rides efficiently and reduce the cost and environmental impact of long-distance travel. Whether you're planning a road trip or just need a shared ride to another city, Hastigo Share makes it simple.
